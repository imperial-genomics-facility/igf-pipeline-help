## What is Globus
Globus is a non-profit service for secure, reliable research data management. With Globus, subscribers can move, share, & discover data via a single interface â€“ whether your files live on a supercomputer, lab cluster, tape archive, public cloud or your laptop, you can manage this data from anywhere, using your existing identities, via just a web browser.
